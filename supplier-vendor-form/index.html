Supplier Vendor Application

Power Plant Services is always ready for opportunity to serve both new and existing customers’ needs, delivering expertise and knowledge where it counts

As part of our supplier quality assurance program, and in accordance with the requirements of our AS9100D & ISO 9001 2015 registration, new and periodic assessments are conducted of our suppliers and sub-contractors. To this end, would you please complete and return the attached vendor questionnaire.

Information from this questionnaire will be used to update our records and enable us to maintain our approved supplier list.

Analysis of this information may result in the need for an assessment or audit by one of our Quality Assurance staff. In this event, we would contact you to arrange a convenient date.

POWER PLANT SERVICES and its employee or assigns will hold the information gathered with this questionnaire in the strictest confidence.
