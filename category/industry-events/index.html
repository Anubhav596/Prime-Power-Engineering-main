PPS is Attending POWERGEN 2022

October 20, 2021/in Industry Events, News /by powerplantservices

Everyone at Power Plant Services (PPS) is looking forward to the opportunity to finally attend POWERGEN 2022. The event will be held January 25-28, 2022 in Dallas, Texas, bringing together more than 15,000 power generation and energy industry professionals.

We look forward to seeing our trusted customers and partners at this exciting event. Visit the POWERGEN conference website at www.powergen.com
